Our
History

Established in the year 2017, Country Cross stands as an active manufacturer and exporters of organic products to different parts of the world. Country Cross specializes in serving natural and organic moringa products in the form of powders and capsules for our customers to experience the unerring benefits of our products. In living this vision, Country Cross products are created by extracting the best out of nature for your true well-being.

What We
Do

Moringa Oleifera is the most widely cultivated species on the arable lands of Tamil Nadu. We stay connected with the environment as well as the farmers right from sowing moringa plants to harvesting and grinding leaves into fine powders. The plants are cultivated on our very owned lands and 50% of the farmers are women who does the process of extracting fresh leaves from the plants, thus uplifting women empowerment and sustainable growth. The dried leaves are then grinded by the machineries. Once the powders are finely grinded it is directly sent for packaging without adding any forms of chemicals or other preservatives.
